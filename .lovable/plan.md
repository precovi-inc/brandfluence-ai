## Phase 3: AI Content Creation Workflow

### Multi-Step Post Creation
1. **Brief** - Post title, platform selection (Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook), creative direction input
2. **Generate** - AI creates multiple content variations using brand voice and guidelines
3. **Craft** - Edit and refine generated content with AI suggestions
4. **Finalize** - Approve and add to schedule or post immediately

### Platform-Specific Optimization
- Automatic character limits and formatting per platform
- AI-tailored tone (casual for Twitter, professional for LinkedIn, visual focus for Instagram)
- Platform-specific best practices applied automatically

### Hashtag & Trend Integration
- AI-suggested hashtags based on content and current trends
- Trending topic recommendations relevant to your brand
- Save and reuse hashtag groups
